Capabilities

Engine Intelligence, Reimagined

Everything you need to monitor, diagnose, and understand your engine — built for engineers, tuned for clarity.

Real-Time Telemetry

16 sensor parameters — RPM, temperatures, load, AFR, oil pressure, battery, vibration, MAP, and more. 6 primary cards plus 10 sidebar readings.

Health Scoring

5-factor diagnostic engine — Engine, Thermal, Fueling, Electrical, and Mechanical health with weighted scoring and letter grades (A–D).

Live Charts

RPM timeline with redline markers, temperature profile, load & throttle curves, and riding mode distribution — all interactive.

Smart Alerts

Threshold-based monitoring — redline warnings, thermal alerts, oil pressure drops, lean/rich mixture, and low battery detection.

CSV Import & Export

Export full session data to CSV with timestamps. Re-import CSVs to replay recorded sessions at adjustable speed.

Physics Simulation

Built-in IC engine sim with realistic RPM curves, thermal dynamics, gear shifting, and 5 riding modes — no hardware required.

Setup

Three Steps. That's It.

From zero to live engine data in under 60 seconds. Or skip straight to simulation — no setup needed.

01

Connect

Plug an ELM327 OBD2 adapter into your vehicle's diagnostic port. USB connection supported.

02

Run Bridge

Double-click ECU_OBD2_Bridge.exe (Windows). Runs silently in the background — no install wizard, no bloat.

03

Monitor

Open the dashboard. Live data flows in automatically. Every sensor, every chart, every alert — in real-time.

Ready to see your engine like never before?

Simulation works instantly in your browser. OBD2 mode available for Windows.
